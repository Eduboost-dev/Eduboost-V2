**POPIA Reference**: Section 32 - Information Officer

## Purpose

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), every public or private body must designate an Information Officer responsible for encouraging compliance with POPIA. This document records the designated Information Officer for EduBoost SA.

### Responsibilities

The Information Officer is responsible for:

- Encouraging compliance with the conditions for the lawful processing of personal information
- Ensuring that a body is aware of its obligations under POPIA
- Dealing with requests from data subjects
- Working with the Information Regulator
- Ensuring that the body has a personal information protection policy
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is available to the public
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is updated regularly
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is communicated to all staff
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is communicated to all data subjects
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is communicated to all third parties
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is communicated to all contractors
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is communicated to all service providers
- Ensuring that the body's personal information protection policy is communicated to all other relevant parties

## Data Subject Rights

Data subjects may contact the Information Officer to:

- Request access to their personal information
- Request correction of their personal information
- Request deletion of their personal information
- Object to processing of their personal information
- Lodge a complaint about the processing of their personal information
- Request information about the processing of their personal information
- Request information about the categories of personal information processed
- Request information about the sources of personal information
- Request information about the purposes of processing
- Request information about the recipients of personal information
- Request information about the retention periods
- Request information about the safeguards applied
- Request information about the international transfers
- Request information about the automated decision-making
- Request information about the profiling

## Escalation

If the Information Officer cannot resolve a data subject's request, the data subject may:

- Contact the Information Regulator: https://www.justice.gov.za/inforeg
- Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator
- Seek legal advice
- Approach the courts
