# Beta Product Scope

Status: proposed controlled-beta scope; pending product approval

## Supported Scope For First Controlled Beta

- Grade: Grade 4
- Subject: Mathematics
- Language: English unless product owner approves additional language support
- User cohort: controlled testers only; no public beta with real learner data until go/no-go approval

## Explicitly Out Of Scope Until Approved

- Production launch
- Public beta with unrestricted real learner data
- Full 120-item production CAPS item-bank coverage
- Paid beta unless billing mode is explicitly approved and tested
- Unverified parent dashboard, PWA/offline sync, and transactional notification claims

## Evidence Required To Expand Scope

- Current CAPS approved item count and educator review evidence
- Answer-key validation plan or reviewer workflow
- Staging smoke evidence
- Legal/security/product sign-off
- Release decision log
