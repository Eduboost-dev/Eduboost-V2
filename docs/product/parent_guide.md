# Parent/Guardian Guide — EduBoost

**Last Updated**: 2026-06-12  
**Audience**: Parents and guardians managing learner accounts

---

## Introduction

As a parent or guardian, you play a central role in your child's learning journey on EduBoost. This guide walks you through managing consent, monitoring progress, and upgrading your account.

---

## Setting Up Your Account

### Creating a Guardian Account

1. Visit [eduboost.co.za](https://eduboost.co.za)
2. Click **Sign Up** → select **Guardian**
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Verify your email address

### Adding a Learner

1. Log in to your guardian account
2. Navigate to **My Learners** → **Add Learner**
3. Enter the learner's details:
   - Full name (as per school records)
   - Date of birth
   - Grade level
   - School name (optional)
4. Click **Add Learner**

## Managing Consent

EduBoost requires parental consent for learners under 18 years old.

### Consent Categories

| Category | Description | Default |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Analytics** | Usage data for product improvement | Opt-in |
| **AI Tutoring** | AI-generated lessons and feedback | Opt-in |
| **RLHF** | Human feedback on AI outputs | Opt-out |
| **Parent Reports** | Progress summaries sent to guardian email | Opt-in |

### How to Manage Consent

1. Log in to your guardian account
2. Go to **Settings** → **Consent Management**
3. Toggle categories on/off as needed
4. Changes take effect immediately

> **Note**: Some categories may be required for specific features. If you disable a required category, the learner's access to certain features may be restricted.

## Monitoring Progress

### Dashboard Overview

The parent dashboard provides:

- **Daily Activity**: Lessons completed, time spent, XP earned
- **Topic Mastery**: Progress across CAPS topics
- **Diagnostic Scores**: Latest assessment results
- **Streaks**: Current learning streak and best streak

### Generating Reports

1. Go to **My Learners** → select learner
2. Click **Generate Report**
3. Choose format: **PDF** or **CSV**
4. Download report

### Setting Alerts

Configure notifications for:

- Weekly progress summary
- Streak at risk (3 days inactive)
- New badge earned
- Topic mastered

Go to **Settings** → **Notifications** to configure.

## Upgrading to Premium

### Benefits of Parent Plan

- Unlimited daily lesson access
- No advertisements
- Detailed progress reports
- Priority support
- Early access to new features

### How to Upgrade

1. Log in to your guardian account
2. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
3. Click **Upgrade to Parent Plan**
4. Enter payment details (Stripe secure checkout)
5. Confirmation email sent

### Cancelling Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until end of billing period

## Data Export and Erasure

### Exporting Learner Data

1. Go to **My Learners** → select learner
2. Click **Export Data**
3. Choose format: **JSON** or **CSV**
4. Download link sent to email

### Requesting Data Erasure

1. Go to **My Learners** → select learner
2. Click **Request Erasure**
3. Confirm the request
4. Erasure processed within 30 days (POPIA requirement)

> **Note**: Data required for legal or financial records may be retained per regulatory requirements.

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot add learner

- Ensure learner's date of birth is correct
- Check that email is not already registered
- Contact support if issues persist

### Consent not saving

- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache

### Payment failed

- Verify card details
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Contact your bank if issue continues
