## General Questions

### What is EduBoost?

EduBoost is an AI-powered adaptive learning platform for South African students in Grades R to 7. We use artificial intelligence to create personalized lessons based on each learner's unique knowledge gaps. Learn more in [Product Overview](product_overview.md).

### How much does EduBoost cost?

EduBoost has three pricing tiers:

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | R0/month | Limited daily lessons (20), basic diagnostics |
| Parent | R149/month | Unlimited lessons, no ads, detailed reports |
| School | R99/student/year | All Parent features + teacher dashboards |

See our [Pricing FAQ](pricing_faq.md) for details.

### What devices does EduBoost work on?

EduBoost works on:

- **Desktop/Laptop**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
- **Tablet**: iPad (Safari), Android tablets (Chrome)
- **Mobile**: Not recommended for learning — use tablet or larger

**Minimum Requirements**:
- Internet speed: 5 Mbps or faster
- Browser: Updated modern browser
- JavaScript: Enabled

## Account & Security

### How do I create an account?

Visit [eduboost.co.za](https://eduboost.co.za) and click **Sign Up**. Choose **Guardian** to create a parent/guardian account, which lets you add learner profiles for your children.

### Is my child's data safe?

Yes. EduBoost is fully POPIA compliant. We:

- Encrypt data in transit and at rest
- Never sell personal data
- Allow data export and deletion on request
- Keep audit logs of all data access

See our [Privacy Policy](https://eduboost.co.za/privacy) for full details.

### Can I see what my child is doing?

Yes. Guardian accounts have full visibility into learner progress, activity logs, and diagnostic results. See our [Parent Guide](parent_guide.md) for details.

## Learning & Content

### What subjects does EduBoost cover?

Currently:

- **Mathematics**: Grades R–7 (CAPS aligned)
- **English**: Grade 1–7 (Home Language)
- **Afrikaans**: Grade 4–7 (First Additional Language)

More subjects coming soon!

### How does the AI generate lessons?

Our AI analyzes diagnostic results to identify knowledge gaps, then generates lessons specifically targeting those gaps. Lessons include:

- Teaching content for the concept
- Practice questions with immediate feedback
- CAPS-aligned examples

AI safety filters prevent inappropriate content from being generated.

### What if my child gets stuck?

Learners can:

- Use **Hint** buttons in lessons
- Review previous lessons
- Ask a parent or teacher for help

Parents can reset specific topics if needed.

## Technical Issues

### EduBoost isn't loading

Try these steps:

1. **Check internet connection**
2. **Refresh the page**
3. **Clear browser cache**
4. **Try a different browser**
5. **Disable ad blockers** (can interfere)

### Lessons won't play audio

- Check device volume
- Ensure headphones are connected (if using)
- Try a different browser

### Payment failed

- Verify card details are correct
- Ensure sufficient funds available
- Contact your bank if issues persist
- Try alternative payment method
