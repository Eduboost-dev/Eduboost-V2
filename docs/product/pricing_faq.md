## Common Questions

### Is there a free trial for Parent Plan?

Yes! New Parent accounts get 7 days of free premium access. No credit card required to start.

### Can I switch between tiers?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time:

- **Upgrade**: Immediate access to new features
- **Downgrade**: Features continue until end of billing period
- **Cancel**: No charge next month

### Do you offer sibling discounts?

Yes! Add 2+ learners to a single guardian account:

- 2 learners: 10% discount
- 3+ learners: 20% discount

Discounts apply automatically in **Settings** → **Subscription**.

### What payment methods do you accept?

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Credit/Debit Card | Visa, Mastercard, Maestro (via Stripe) |
| Instant EFT | Fica, Nedbank, ABSA |
| Debit Order | South African bank accounts only |

## School Pricing

### How is school pricing calculated?

Pricing is per student per year:

- **Base**: R99/student/year
- **Minimum**: 20 students
- **Maximum**: No limit

Example: 50 students = R4,950/year (R412.50/month)

### What's included in school pricing?

- All Parent Plan features for each student
- Teacher accounts (unlimited)
- Class analytics dashboard
- Bulk student import (CSV)
- Teacher training (2 hours virtual)
- Dedicated support channel

### Do you offer discounts for NGOs?

Yes! Registered non-profits receive 30% discount. Contact **schools@eduboost.co.za** with your NPO number.

## Billing

### When am I charged?

- **Monthly**: Same date each month
- **School**: Annual upfront

### Where can I see my invoices?

Log in → **Settings** → **Billing** → **View Invoices**

### What is your refund policy?

- **Within 14 days**: Full refund if no lessons completed
- **After 14 days**: No refund for current period
- **School**: Pro-rata refunds for early termination

## Tax & VAT

All prices include VAT (15%). Tax invoices are available in **Settings** → **Billing** → **Invoices**.
