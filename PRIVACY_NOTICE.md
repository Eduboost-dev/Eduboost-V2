# EduBoost SA Privacy Notice

**Last Updated**: 2026-05-28
**Version**: 1.0
**Applicable Law**: Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) 4 of 2013

## Introduction

EduBoost SA ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of learners, guardians, and other data subjects who use our educational platform. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

## Who We Are

EduBoost SA is an educational technology company providing adaptive learning solutions for South African learners. We process personal information to deliver personalized educational experiences, track learning progress, and improve our services.

## Information We Collect

### Learner Information

We collect the following information about learners:

- **Personal Details**: Name, grade, language preference
- **Learning Data**: Diagnostic results, lesson progress, practice performance, mastery snapshots, spaced review schedules
- **Engagement Data**: Time spent, interaction patterns, response accuracy
- **Device Information**: Browser type, device type, IP address (hashed)

### Guardian Information

We collect the following information about guardians:

- **Contact Details**: Email address, display name
- **Account Information**: Subscription tier, account status
- **Consent Records**: Consent history, consent version, consent state

### Technical Information

We collect the following technical information:

- **Usage Data**: Session logs, error reports, performance metrics
- **Analytics Data**: Learning analytics, engagement metrics, usage patterns

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes

We use personal information for the following primary purposes:

1. **Educational Service Delivery**: Provide adaptive learning experiences, personalized lesson recommendations, and progress tracking
2. **Account Management**: Create and manage learner and guardian accounts
3. **Consent Management**: Track parental consent status and consent versioning
4. **Learning Analytics**: Analyze learning patterns to improve educational outcomes
5. **Service Improvement**: Enhance platform features and user experience
6. **Safety and Security**: Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
7. **Legal Compliance**: Meet obligations under POPIA and other applicable laws

### AI and Adaptive Learning

Our platform uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to:

- Personalize learning paths based on individual performance
- Recommend appropriate lessons and practice exercises
- Adjust difficulty levels to match learner ability
- Identify knowledge gaps and suggest remediation
- Optimize spaced review schedules for long-term retention

AI processing is conducted with appropriate safeguards to ensure fairness, transparency, and accountability.

## Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal information based on the following lawful bases under POPIA:

1. **Consent**: For processing that requires explicit consent from guardians
2. **Legitimate Interests**: For processing necessary for our legitimate business interests
3. **Contractual Necessity**: For processing necessary to provide our services
4. **Legal Obligation**: For processing required by law

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information to third parties. We may share personal information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- **Safety**: To protect the rights, property, or safety of EduBoost SA, our users, or the public

All third-party data sharing is conducted under appropriate data processing agreements.

## International Data Transfers

We do not transfer personal information outside of South Africa without appropriate safeguards in accordance with POPIA requirements.

## Data Retention

We retain personal information for the following periods:

- **Learner Data**: Retained for the duration of the learner's active use of the platform, plus any applicable legal retention periods
- **Guardian Data**: Retained for the duration of the guardian's account, plus any applicable legal retention periods
- **Audit Records**: Retained for 7 years in accordance with POPIA requirements
- **Consent Records**: Retained indefinitely to demonstrate compliance with consent requirements

Upon request, we will delete personal information in accordance with our erasure procedures, subject to legal and operational constraints.

## Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the following rights:

### Right to Access

You may request access to your personal information by contacting us. We will provide a comprehensive record of all personal information we hold about you or your child.

### Right to Correction

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information. We will update our records accordingly.

### Right to Deletion (Erasure)

You may request deletion of your personal information. We will process erasure requests in accordance with our erasure workflow, which includes:

- Verification of your identity and authorization
- Safety checks to ensure deletion is appropriate
- Grace period for reconsideration
- Physical deletion of personal information
- Preservation of audit records
- Anonymization of aggregate metrics

### Right to Object to Processing

You may object to the processing of your personal information on legitimate grounds. We will assess your objection and cease processing unless we have compelling legitimate grounds.

### Right to Withdraw Consent

Guardians may withdraw consent for their child's data processing at any time. Withdrawal will:

- Immediately stop new data processing for the learner
- Offer the option to export existing data before deletion
- Offer the option to request erasure of existing data
- Create an audit record of the withdrawal

### Right to Lodge a Complaint

You may lodge a complaint with us or with the Information Regulator if you believe your personal information has been processed in violation of POPIA.

### Right to Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

## Guardian Consent

We require parental consent before processing learner personal information. Consent includes:

- **Consent Version**: We track the version of the privacy policy under which consent was granted
- **Consent Expiry**: Consent expires after a defined period and must be renewed
- **Consent Withdrawal**: Guardians may withdraw consent at any time
- **Consent History**: We maintain a complete history of consent grants, renewals, and withdrawals

## Security Safeguards

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including:

- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit who can access personal information
- **Audit Logging**: All access to personal information is logged for accountability
- **Security Testing**: Regular security testing and vulnerability assessments
- **Incident Response**: Procedures for detecting and responding to security incidents

## Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may result in a risk of harm to data subjects, we will:

- Assess the severity of the breach
- Notify affected data subjects without undue delay
- Notify the Information Regulator where required
- Take steps to mitigate the harm
- Document the breach and our response

## Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for use by minors under the supervision of guardians. We:

- Require parental consent before processing learner data
- Do not collect more information than necessary for educational purposes
- Provide guardians with tools to manage their child's data
- Implement additional safeguards for children's data

## Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. We will notify data subjects of material changes by:

- Posting the updated notice on our website
- Sending an email notification to registered guardians
- Updating the consent version and requesting renewal
